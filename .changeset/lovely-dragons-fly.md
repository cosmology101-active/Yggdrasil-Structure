# Yggdrasil 6 — Vanaheim

A massive update introducing **Vanaheim**, the 4th realm of the pack. Yggdrasil Alternative 6 is also available simultaneously.

## Vanaheim

A brand new structure with a unique design — the pattern and room placement stays identical every visit, but the rooms themselves are different.

**Structure layout:**
1. A hole visible from the surface
2. A vertical then horizontal tunnel
3. A labyrinth section
4. The Vanaheim dwelling — a massive 100x100 room
5. Side rooms connected to the main area

A guardian awaits at its center with intermediate difficulty. Each chest, vault, and spawner is placed at strategic spots for enjoyable progression. Spawners summon different creature types for combat variety.

**Rewards:**
- A new exclusive enchantment via NeoEnchant — **Seiors Oblivions**
- Eggs for Cow, Pig, Chicken and Sheep

## Folkvangr

Vanaheim's stele — appears more frequently and provides a map to locate Vanaheim. Significantly larger than previous steles, pushing immersion to the maximum.

## Reva Rework

Reva is no longer a familiar you can activate. It's now a **stone containing its soul** that grants **fire immunity**, for better stability across server implementations.

## Partial Alternative Merge

- **Runic Dimension removed** — structures relocated to the Nether
- **Nether Alfheim Tree** replaces Runic Alfheim Tree (Nether roof access)
- **Labyrinth**, **Cubeoid** and **Dokkalfar Ruins** now available in the Nether
- **Asflors quest** updated to remove Runic Dimension references
- **Floating island** now includes a Nether portal instead
- **Divine Keys** and their villagers removed

## Advancements System

Each structure you discover unlocks an advancement. Track which structures you've explored and which ones you're still missing. Legendary items are also registered.

## 6.0.1 — Multi-Version & Polish

- **14 structures updated** with visual improvements
- **All console warnings and errors removed**
- **Multi-version support** — versions 1.21.5 through 1.21.11 use the same zip file
- Available for 1.21, 1.21.1, and 1.21.5 to 1.21.11

## Migration Warning

> **Warning:** Don't update on existing worlds without preparation. The Runic Dimension removal and Reva behavior change may cause migration issues. Ask for help on Discord.

Future updates won't have compatibility issues with Paper/Bukkit and modpacks. Removing Yggdrasil won't cause any problems anymore!
